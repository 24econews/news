### [Electricity and gas subsidy: step by step, how to apply and who can access the benefit in June](https://www.infobae.com/economia/2026/06/06/subsidio-a-la-luz-y-al-gas-paso-a-paso-como-pedirlo-y-quienes-pueden-acceder-al-beneficio-en-junio/)

The Government launched in 2026 the Focused Energy Subsidies (SEF), a new regime that unifies benefits for electricity, natural gas, piped propane gas, and 10-kilo gas canisters under a single eligibility criterion. To access them, household income must not exceed the equivalent of three Total Basic Baskets according to Indec, although exceptions are also considered for holders of the Unique Disability Certificate (CUD) and people registered in ReNaBaP. The scheme replaces the previous RASE and eliminates segmentation by levels, leaving only two categories: beneficiaries and excluded. Data of those already registered was automatically migrated to the new Registry of Focused Energy Subsidies (ReSEF), while new applicants must process it through the argentina.gob.ar/subsidios platform, with a Mi Argentina account.

General Resolution (ARCA) 5.848, published in the Official Gazette on May 18 and aligned with Law 27.802 on Labor Modernization, formalized the implementation of the Digital Work Certificate, modifying article 80 of the Employment Contract Law. The measure is part of a broader process of comprehensive digitization of labor, social security, and tax information, which includes new electronic receipt schemes and even reaches the regime for private household workers. The change redefines the role of the State, which goes from collecting to cross-checking, validating, and controlling data in real time, requiring employers to have greater consistency in the management of their information. For companies, this means reviewing their administrative processes and adapting to a scheme where the labor certificate ceases to be an exit procedure and becomes part of a permanent oversight system.
