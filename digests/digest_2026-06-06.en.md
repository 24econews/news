### [Electricity and gas subsidies: step by step, how to request them and who can access the benefit in June](https://www.infobae.com/economia/2026/06/06/subsidio-a-la-luz-y-al-gas-paso-a-paso-como-pedirlo-y-quienes-pueden-acceder-al-beneficio-en-junio/)

In 2026, the Government launched the Focused Energy Subsidies (SEF) regime, which unifies benefits for electricity, natural gas, piped propane gas, and 10-kilo gas cylinders under a single eligibility criterion. To qualify, household income must not exceed the equivalent of three Total Basic Baskets as measured by Indec, although exceptions are made for holders of the Unique Disability Certificate (CUD) and households registered in the ReNaBaP. The system eliminates segmentation by levels and establishes only two categories: beneficiaries and excluded, replacing previous schemes such as the Social Gas Tariff and the Hogar Program. The procedure is carried out exclusively digitally at argentina.gob.ar/subsidios, through a Mi Argentina account, and those registered in the former RASE were automatically migrated to the new Focused Energy Subsidies Registry (ReSEF).

General Resolution (ARCA) 5.848, published on May 18 and aligned with Law 27.802 on Labor Modernization, implemented the Digital Work Certificate, modifying article 80 of the Labor Contract Law. The measure is part of a broader process of comprehensive digitalization of labor, pension, and tax information, which includes new electronic receipt schemes and even extends to the domestic workers regime. The change redefines the role of the State, which shifts from collecting to cross-referencing, validating, and controlling data in real time, requiring greater consistency from employers in managing their information. For companies, this means reviewing internal processes and adapting to a permanent oversight scheme that replaces the traditional after-the-fact control based on in-person procedures.
